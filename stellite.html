Elgiloy

Elgiloy Alloys for Critical Applications

Elgiloy alloys are a family of austenitic nickel-chromium-based superalloys that are known for their outstanding resistance to oxidation and corrosion at high temperatures. These materials are primarily used in applications requiring high strength and oxidation resistance in environments that exceed 1,000°F (537°C).

Key Properties:

  • High Temperature Strength
  • Corrosion and Oxidation Resistance
  • Excellent Weldability
  • Thermal Fatigue Resistance
  • Resistance to Scaling and Sulfidation

Applications:

  • Aerospace and Aviation
  • Power Generation
  • Chemical Processing
  • Marine and Offshore
