Nichrome Alloys

Nichrome Alloys for Critical Applications

Nichrome alloys are a family of austenitic nickel-chromium-based superalloys that are known for their outstanding resistance to oxidation and corrosion at high temperatures. These materials are primarily used in applications requiring high strength and oxidation resistance in environments that exceed 1,000°F (537°C).

Key Properties:

  • High Temperature Strength
  • Corrosion and Oxidation Resistance
  • Excellent Weldability
  • Thermal Fatigue Resistance
  • Resistance to Scaling and Sulfidation

Applications:

  • Aerospace and Aviation
  • Power Generation
  • Chemical Processing
  • Marine and Offshore

Available Grades:

  • NiCr 80:20
  • NiCr 70:30
  • NiCr 60:15
  • NiCr 40:20
  • NiCr 30:20
  • NiCr 20:25
